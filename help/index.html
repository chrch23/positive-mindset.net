Support for Positive Mindset apps

If you need help with your subscription or have a support request, use one of the options below.

How to cancel a subscription

Subscriptions are managed by Apple through your App Store account.

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone.
  2. Tap your Apple ID at the top.
  3. Tap Subscriptions.
  4. Select the subscription for the app.
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription.

If you do not see the subscription there, it may already be cancelled or it may have been purchased with a different Apple ID.

How to request a refund

Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple.

  1. Open Apple’s problem reporting page in your browser.
  2. Sign in with the Apple ID used for the purchase.
  3. Find the subscription or purchase for the app.
  4. Choose Request a refund.
  5. Select a reason and submit your request.

Apple reviews refund requests directly and decides the outcome.
